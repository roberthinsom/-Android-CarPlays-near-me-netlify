Apple CarPlay Installation Services

What is Apple CarPlay?
Apple CarPlay is a system that allows you to connect your iPhone to your vehicle's infotainment system, offering a range of features such as navigation, music, messaging, and more. With Apple CarPlay, you can:

  • Get directions with Apple Maps
  • Make hands-free calls
  • Stream music or podcasts from Apple Music
  • Send and receive messages with Siri

Android Auto Installation Services

What is Android Auto?
Android Auto offers similar features to Apple CarPlay, but it is designed specifically for Android smartphone users. This system connects your Android phone to your car's infotainment system, providing access to apps like Google Maps, Spotify, Google Assistant, and more. With Android Auto, you can:

  • Get navigation and traffic updates in real-time with Google Maps
  • Stream music and podcasts from apps like Spotify, YouTube Music, and others
  • Make hands-free calls and send messages with Google Assistant
